# Spring Gardens Care Guide

A shared care guide for the four flagship flowers at **Spring Gardens**:
[roses](roses.md), [tulips](tulips.md), [sunflowers](sunflowers.txt),
and daisies. **Maria Alvarez** uses this routine for every bed.

## Watering

| Flower      | Spring        | Summer        | Autumn       |
| ----------- | ------------- | ------------- | ------------ |
| Roses       | 2× per week   | 3× per week   | 1× per week  |
| Tulips      | At planting   | Stored bulbs  | Stored bulbs |
| Sunflowers  | At sowing     | 1× per week   | 1× per week  |
| Daisies     | 1× per week   | 2× per week   | As needed    |

## Feeding

Roses get a balanced rose feed every six weeks; tulips need none;
sunflowers benefit from a liquid feed once when buds form; daisies
thrive in poor soil and should not be fed.

## Pests

- **Aphids** — sprayed with diluted soap for roses; companion garlic
  helps too.
- **Slugs** — wool pellets around tulip and daisy beds.
- **Birds** — netting over sunflower seed heads in late summer.

See [`catalog.json`](catalog.json) for variety-specific notes.
