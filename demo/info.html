Spring Gardens

Welcome to Spring Gardens, the four-acre demonstration garden tended by Maria Alvarez. This leaflet links to everything you need to plan your visit.

Featured flowers

  • Roses — including New Dawn, Crimson Glory, Iceberg, and Lady of Shalott.
  • Tulips — including Queen of Night, Apricot Beauty, and Spring Green.
  • Sunflowers — including Russian Mammoth and Velvet Queen.
  • Daisies — Pomponette and Tasso Pink, lining every border.
